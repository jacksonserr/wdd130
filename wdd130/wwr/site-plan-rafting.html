Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to show the public the joys of White Water Rafting. We have been a tour company for 10 years and have shared the joy to thousands of satisfied customers who are now life long fans of the activity.

Audience

Our Target Audience is anyone who is willing to learn how to White Water Raft! 1.What if we don't know anything about white water rafting? Well we have excellent river tour guides who can join any adventure to teach and make sure everyone is happy and safe. 2.Can my kids join if they do not know how to swim? We include life jackets with every tour package and other saftey features in order to keep our guests safe. 3.Is there any age limit at all? The only limit is yourself but we strive to include any guest who wants to be included!
